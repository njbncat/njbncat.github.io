About Me

Hi there, my name is Nick Boutross and I am a senior here at North Carolina A&T. I plan to graduate in December of this year.

Currently, I works in telecommunications and previously worked in the biopharaceutical industry. Prior to starting my professional career, I served in the United States Marine Corps for 5 years obtaining the rank of sergeant. I spent 2 years of that service in Okinawa Japan with a tour to South Korea and the remainder in Cherry Point NC where I had 1 tour to Afghanistan.

I have been married for nearly seven years to my loving wife who is constantly pushing me forward. I spend my time away from the computer at parks, in the yard, or on a trail, usually with my phone in hand to take pictures.

On nights without assignment work to accomplish I like to play Destiny, but those nights are few and far between with other committments.
